Bulking

Bulking is a phase in fitness focused on gaining muscle mass by eating in a calorie surplus—meaning you consume more calories than your body burns each day. The goal is to fuel your body with enough energy and nutrients to support muscle growth, especially when paired with consistent strength training. This is suggested for people who are skinny and want to gain muscle mass. If you want more information on bulking you can visit our page below:Bulking Info

Cutting

Cutting is the phase in a fitness journey focused on losing body fat while preserving as much lean muscle mass as possible. It usually follows a bulking phase and helps reveal the muscle you've built by reducing overall body fat.The main strategy behind cutting is to eat in a calorie deficit—consuming fewer calories than your body burns—while maintaining a solid workout routine, especially resistance training.If you want more information on cutting you can visit this our below:Cutting Info
